Welcome to the Drexel Co-op Portal,

The Drexel Co-op Portal is your gateway to finding and applying for professional work experiences that complement your academic studies. Through this system, students can explor available co-op positions upload and upate their resumes, and apply for jobs during their assigned cycle (Fall/Winter or Spring/Summer). The portal helps you manage every step of the process, from searching for opportunities to submitting applications and tracking employer responses.
